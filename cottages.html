Dhanalakshmi

Dhanalakshmi, the Goddess of wealth, symbolizes prosperity and abundance. This cottage guides you on a path to financial success and the fulfillment of your desires.

Aadhi Lakshmi

Goddess Aadhi Lakshmi, the primordial form of Lakshmi, is the origin of all existence and the force behind creation. Her presence brings happiness and bliss to all.

Veera Lakshmi

Goddess Veera Lakshmi represents bravery, strength, and valor. Also known as Dhairya Lakshmi, she provides the courage to face life's challenges.

Dhanya Lakshmi

Dhanya Lakshmi, the Goddess of Grains, is the provider of agricultural wealth and ensures there is enough food for all, eliminating hunger.

Santhana Lakshmi

Santhana Lakshmi symbolizes fertility and blesses couples with children. She contributes to the overall well-being of the child.

Gaja Lakshmi

Gaja Lakshmi, signifying royalty and power. She provides abundance and prosperity, making her a revered presence in many households.

Vijayalakshmi

Goddess Vijayalakshmi embodies victory, not only in battles but also in life's endeavors. She leads us to triumph in all aspects of existence.

Aishwarya Lakshmi

Aishwarya Lakshmi bestows opulence in all its forms, surpassing material luxuries to guide you toward ultimate inner bliss.
