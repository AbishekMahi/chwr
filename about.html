Our Story

Rediscover Opulence, Sustainability, and Inner Peace

Chettinad Heritage & Wellness Resorts, nestled in the heart of Tamil Nadu, is a testament to the grandeur of Chettinad architecture, meticulously crafted with a deep commitment to sustainability. This resort, located in Mettupatti Shri Pillaiyar Kovil, Nachandupatti, Pudukkottai, Tamil Nadu, is the brainchild of Selvam Alagappan Chettiyar, who drew inspiration from his 160-year-old traditional house.

What sets us apart is our unwavering dedication to preserving the essence of Chettinad heritage. Not a single tree was felled during the construction of this resort. Instead, it's a true embodiment of our respect for nature and tradition.


Immersive Experiences Await

Our resort offers a range of accommodations, from the cozy Bangala Comfort rooms to the sweeping vistas from the Bangala Suite. Wander through our Bangala Valavu atrium, a tribute to traditional Chettinad architecture, and explore the Lakshmi Cottages, each celebrating one of the eight forms of Goddess Lakshmi. For a truly immersive experience, choose the Marakalam, offering a window view of the picturesque Mettupatti reservoir.

As you enter the resort, you're greeted by the enchanting sight of the Tamarai Kenaru, reflecting the timeless beauty of the surroundings. Explore our farm, pluck fresh vegetables, and connect with nature. Take a stroll through the Panchatatva Walk, celebrating the five elements of nature, and let it energize your mind, body, and soul.

Culinary Delights

At our restaurant, we pride ourselves on serving authentic Chettinad cuisine, prepared without artificial additives. Every dish is a celebration of traditional Chettinad flavors, lovingly handcrafted by skilled chefs who use locally-sourced ingredients. The result is a culinary journey that's not only delicious but visually captivating.

Sustainability and Organic Living

Chettinad Heritage & Wellness Resorts seamlessly combines grandeur heritage with the allure of sustainability. Our eco-conscious living initiatives include organic farming, where our lush green gardens yield a bountiful harvest of organic vegetables. You'll also encounter native breeds of chickens and Punganur Kuttai Madu, a local cattle variety, freely gracing our lush pastures, providing an authentic farm-to-table experience.

Discover the opulence, sustainability, and inner peace at Chettinad Heritage & Wellness Resorts, where every element of our creation is designed to energize your heart and soul. Welcome to a resort that seamlessly blends heritage with the allure of sustainability.
