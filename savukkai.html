SAVUKKAI - OUR WELLNESS CENTRE

At Chettinad Heritage & Wellness Resorts, we introduce you to Savukkai, a sanctuary of well-being deeply rooted in Chettinad's age-old traditions. The name 'Savukkai' itself is derived from the Tamil word 'Savukkiiyam,' a fundamental element of Chettinad's palatial living. Here, we draw upon the time-honored practices of the Nagarathar Chettiars, who embraced the art of self-care through periodic oil massages and wellness rituals.

Savukkai is a testament to our commitment to holistic well-being. At its heart lies an eight-shaped water pedestal, a symbol of blessings from Almighty Shri Dhanvantri, the God of Health. This divine grace extends to heal the eight chakras of the human body, fostering rejuvenation, balance, and serenity.

Our Array of Healing Modalities

Savukkai offers a spectrum of healing practices, guided by an in-house Doctor and expert professionals. Our focus extends to:

YOGA

Embark on a journey of self-discovery with yoga. This ancient practice enhances strength, flexibility, and vitality. It not only keeps you limber and energized but also fosters mental clarity, focus, and an overall sense of well-being.

DIET THERAPY

Our carefully curated meal plans control the intake of specific foods and nutrients, ensuring your body receives the nourishment it deserves.

ACUPUNCTURE

Harness the power of acupuncture, where fine needles are strategically inserted into the skin. This practice promotes holistic wellness, aids stress management, and provides effective pain relief.

MASSAGE THERAPY

Indulge in the soothing embrace of massage therapy, where skilled hands knead, rub, tap, and stroke to relieve stress, enhance circulation, lower blood pressure, and promote relaxation.

FISH PEDICURE

Experience a unique foot treatment that leaves your skin feeling soft and rejuvenated, a perfect blend of indulgence and well-being.

PANCHAKARMA

Our Panchakarma treatments, comprising five methods, aim to cleanse your body of unwanted waste, offering preventive, curative, and promotive actions for various ailments.

BANANA LEAF BATH

Unlock the healing potential of banana leaves with this restorative therapy. The green color of banana leaves safeguards against harmful rays and toxins, allowing energy to flow throughout your body.

VARMAM THERAPY

Explore the manual stimulation of vital life energy points through various manipulation techniques, enhancing your overall well-being.

MUD BATH

The age-old wisdom of the Vedas comes to life through our mud bath therapy, a practice known for its toxin-absorbing properties. It provides cooling and relaxation, holding moisture for an extended period.

Embrace the wisdom of ancient healing practices at Savukkai, where each modality weaves a tapestry of well-being for your body and soul. Your path to inner peace and rejuvenation begins here.
