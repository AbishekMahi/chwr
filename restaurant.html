Our Restaurant

Step into the heart of Chettinad flavors at Our Restaurant, a culinary journey that celebrates the region's rich and diverse cuisine. Crafted by skilled Chettinad chefs, our menu is a homage to locally-sourced ingredients, prepared with love and care.

Indulge in mouthwatering dishes that not only tantalize your taste buds but also nurture your well-being, as we embrace the tradition of using nature's own spices and seasonings. Each meal is a work of art, not just in taste but also in presentation, ensuring your dining experience is a visual delight.

Key Highlights:

  1. Authentic Chettinad cuisine
  2. Locally-sourced, fresh ingredients
  3. Traditional and contemporary dishes
  4. Visual culinary artistry
  5. Exquisite dining ambiance

Personalized Catering for Special Events:

At Our Restaurant, we offer the unique opportunity to book food for your special events, whether hosted within the resort or at external venues. You have the flexibility to curate your own custom menu or choose from our diverse selection.

Developed Menu:

Our extensive menu offers a blend of traditional and contemporary dishes, ensuring there's something to delight every palate. From aromatic curries to savory snacks and delectable desserts, our culinary creations are a true reflection of Chettinad's culinary heritage.

Whether it's a romantic dinner for two, a family celebration, or a grand event, Our Restaurant promises an unforgettable culinary experience. Satisfy your hunger for exceptional food and immerse yourself in the regional essence, tailored to your preferences.
