Our Restaurant

Our wellness centre - Savukkai - is named after the tamil word Savukkiiyam. Savukkai forms an integral part of the traditional Chettinad palatial living, where the Chettiars used to heal themselves with periodical oil massages and wellness care. We have replicated the healthy living concepts of the yesteryear Nagarathar Chettiars to present Savukkai - our Wellness Haven.

The healing power within this Wellness resort is empowered with the blessings of Almighty Shri Dhanvantri - the God of Health, instituted at the center of an 8 shaped water filled pedestal, denoting His powerful grace to heal over the 8 chakras of the human body. The treatment comprising of various methods and packages, comes with due support of an in-house Doctor and well trained professionals.
