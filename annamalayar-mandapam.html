Annamalayar Mandapam

Step into the grandeur of Annamalayar Mandapam, a magnificent hall space that can accommodate up to 150 guests. Whether it's a wedding, a corporate event, or any special celebration, our beautifully designed mandapam sets the stage for unforgettable gatherings. Revel in the opulence as you create cherished moments within its elegant confines.

Annamalaiyar Mandapam is not just a grand hall; it's a sacred space dedicated to Lord Annamalaiyar, offering a serene backdrop for your cherished gatherings and celebrations. Nestled within our remarkable Chettinad palace, it's not just a place to stay but an embodiment of Chettinad's rich cultural heritage and culinary delights. Experience an oasis where moments of happiness and cultural immersion unite in perfect harmony. Your celebrations, our dedication to excellence!

Features:

  1. Accommodates up to 150 guests
  2. Ideal for weddings, corporate events, and special occasions
  3. Exquisite Chettinad architecture
  4. Impeccable service and facilities to elevate your event
