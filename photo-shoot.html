PHOTO SHOOT: Capturing Heritage Beauty

Unveil the charm of our heritage architecture and picturesque layout, offering the perfect canvas for capturing timeless moments. Our property provides an exquisite backdrop for photo shoots, ensuring each snapshot is a work of art. From pre-wedding photo shoots to fashion shoots, our surroundings are an invitation to create visual stories that resonate with the beauty of Chettinad.

Tarrif
  1. Starting from a minimum of 4 hours
  2. Rs 1500/- per hour
  3. GST Taxes extra

Key Highlights:

  1. Heritage architecture: Experience the timeless beauty of Chettinad's architectural marvels.
  2. Scenic Layout: The lush surroundings offer a harmonious blend of natural and man-made beauty.
  3. Versatile Setting: Suitable for pre-wedding, fashion, or any thematic photo shoots.
  4. Dress Changing Room: We provide convenient changing rooms to accommodate outfit changes.
  5. Professional Support: Our team is on hand to assist with all your requirements.
  6. Lighting Options: Explore natural and ambient lighting for the perfect shot.

Capture the essence of Chettinad's architectural grandeur, where every click is a brushstroke painting a picture of timeless elegance. Your photographic journey begins here, and every frame is a story waiting to be told.
