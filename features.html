Explore Our Features

Panchatatva Walk

Explore our Panchatatva Walk, a tranquil pathway symbolizing the five elements of nature: Air, Fire, Water, Earth, and Universe. This unique experience is designed to harmonize your mind, body, and soul.

Oorani - Inspiring Tranquility

Dive into serenity at Oorani, our picturesque swimming pool that inspires relaxation with its breathtaking ambiance. Unwind in the midst of nature's beauty.

Tiruchendur Subramania Swamy Gaushala

Connect with divine energy at our sacred home for native breed cows. Feel the positivity within as you engage with these revered animals in a tranquil setting.

Indoor Games

Engage your mind and spirit with traditional games like Pallanguli, Chess, and Carrom. Enjoy a perfect blend of relaxation and recreation.

Farm-To-Table

Experience the farm-to-table concept as you wander through our lush farm. Handpick your own vegetables, ensuring a fresh and authentic culinary experience.

Tanjore Art Gallery

Explore our exclusive gallery for a curated shopping experience. Discover exquisite treasures that reflect the rich heritage of Tanjore art.

Chettinad Heritage
& Wellness Resorts

Chettinad Heritage & Wellness Resorts is a serene sanctuary, nestled in the heart of Chettinad, dedicated to preserving the opulence of Chettinad Architecture and culture while offering modern comforts and a unique photographic experience.
